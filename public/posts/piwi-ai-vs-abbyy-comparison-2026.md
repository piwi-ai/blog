## What Piwi.ai Does Differently

**Intelligent Mapping, Zero Setup.** Legacy tools often require you to build templates and train models to show the system exactly where to extract data. Piwi.ai uses semantic AI to understand the document instantly. It extracts the data and maps it directly into your finalized PDF templates automatically.

**Finished Documents in Minutes.** With Piwi.ai, you aren't just extracting raw data strings. You create an account, upload your source documents (like an ID and a deed), choose your output template, and download the finished, signature-ready document. 

**Ready for Any Scale.** Built for ease of use, Piwi.ai allows an office manager to start automating documents in an afternoon. But it also has the robust security, compliance, and custom contract options required by large enterprises processing documents at scale.

**Cross-document validation.** Before any output is generated, Piwi.ai checks data consistency across all your source documents. If the name on a passport doesn't match the utility bill, the system flags it before you send anything out.
